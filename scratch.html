Personal Finance Overview

Do you have your finances in order? This page can help teach you the basics that will lead you to financial freedom.

First, go through this list and determine which steps you are already doing.

  1. Income
    • How much money do you make?
    • Can you be making more money?
    • Are you happy with your job?
  2. Expenses
    • How much money do you spend each week?
    • What percent of your income do you save?
    • How can you reduce your expenses?
  3. Retirement
    • Do you have a retirement account with your job?
    • What is your contribution plan?
    • Are you maxing out your contributions?
